# Cryptocurrency Portfolio Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the performance of a cryptocurrency portfolio consisting of Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Solana (SOL). The dashboard provides insights into price movements, market capitalization, trading volume, and portfolio allocation over time.

## Objectives

* Track the historical performance of major cryptocurrencies.
* Compare price trends of BTC, ETH, and SOL.
* Analyze market capitalization and trading volume.
* Understand portfolio diversification and risk.
* Generate actionable insights for investors.

## Tools Used

* Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization
* Microsoft Excel / CSV – Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Cryptocurrency historical data from Kaggle / Yahoo Finance

## Dashboard Features

### KPIs

* Latest Closing Price
* Market Capitalization
* Trading Volume
* Price Change (%)

### Visualizations

* Line Chart: Historical price trends of BTC, ETH, and SOL
* Bar Chart: Market capitalization comparison
* Area Chart: Trading volume over time
* Donut Chart: Portfolio allocation
* Slicers: Cryptocurrency and Date filters

## Key Insights

* Bitcoin maintains the highest market capitalization and shows relatively stable long-term growth.
* Ethereum demonstrates consistent growth and strong market presence due to its smart contract ecosystem.
* Solana exhibits higher price volatility but offers significant growth potential.
* Portfolio diversification across BTC, ETH, and SOL helps balance risk and return.
* Trading volumes tend to increase during periods of major price fluctuations, indicating higher market activity.

## Conclusion

The Cryptocurrency Portfolio Analysis dashboard provides a comprehensive view of market trends and portfolio performance. By comparing major cryptocurrencies and analyzing key financial indicators, investors can make more informed decisions and better understand the dynamics of the crypto market.
